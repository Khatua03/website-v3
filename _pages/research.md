# Current Research

## Ion Cyclotron Emission in Ohmic Tokamak Plasmas

![ICE in MAST-U](/assets/images/research/ice_mastu.jpg)

Ion Cyclotron Emission (ICE) is traditionally associated with energetic ions generated by auxiliary heating systems. My current work investigates the surprising observation of ICE in Ohmic MAST-U plasmas and its relationship with plasma stability, turbulence, and confinement.

**Related Publications**
- APS-DPP 2025: Observation of Ion Cyclotron Emission in MAST-U Ohmic Plasma

---

## Doppler Backscattering Diagnostics for Fusion Plasmas

![DBS System](/assets/images/research/dbs_system.jpg)

Development of multi-frequency Q-band and V-band Doppler Backscattering diagnostics for localized turbulence and flow measurements in spherical tokamaks.

**Key Topics**
- Turbulence measurements
- Flow velocity diagnostics
- Millimeter-wave instrumentation
- Plasma fluctuations
